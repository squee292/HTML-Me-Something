Preping the model

When starting a new miniature, you first must remove the pieces from a sprue. Below is an image of what the sprue looks like.

After removing the pieces I would then glue them together to get them ready to prime and paint.

Assembiling and Painting

After assembling the pieces, I then would prime the model and get them ready to paint. I start with a base color,
in this case brown as the model is mostly wood. I then painted the metal parts a burnish gold so that when I shade
the model, they turn out a bronze color.

I then used a technique called dry brushing to highlight the grains
in the wood so they stand out more.
